About Me

It's a joke in my family that I'm a "Professional Student"... I love to learn new things and like a challenge. It is one concept I want to impart to my children - that learning never stops. I have done many things, but some of the more interesting and rewarding things I have done to date have been earning my Commercial pilots license with an Instrument rating in rotorcraft (helicopters), obtaining a class B CDL, and earning an AAS degree with a concentration in Anthropology.

I have also done a variety of things for employment as well. While most of the work I've been involved in has been minimum-wage customer service type work, I have also had more engaging work as a tutor for the Surgical Technologist program at Renton Technical College, and as a security officer with the TSA.

I have many interests - I love reading Sci-fi and fantasy novels, and like to swim and hike. Now if only there were more hours in a day to do it all...
